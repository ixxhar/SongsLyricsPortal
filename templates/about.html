About Me
Izhar Hussain

This web app is developed as a final year project by Izhar Hussain, the application of this website is to use different information retrieval techniques for the recommendations of music through lyrics for a certain query. The algorithms used for this project are Cosine Similarity, BM25 and WordNet. The Project is supervised by Dr. Shahzad Faisal, a great effort in terms of time and energy was put into this project by the developer, also superviser has been supportive and cooperative.
